Q. What makes you feel happy?

I am happiest
when I am playing the rindik.

STEP 01

全文を聞こう

まずは止めずに最後まで聞いて、全体の流れをつかもう。

I am happiest when I am playing the rindik.
I enjoy listening to gamelan music in my village because the sound of the rindik makes me feel calm and happy.
Sometimes I feel nostalgic, and even tears come to my eyes.
I can’t live without it now.
Playing the rindik on a quiet night really relaxes me.

STEP 02

チャンクごとに滑らかに読めるように練習しよう

全文音声から切り出した音声を聞いて、同じリズムで読もう。

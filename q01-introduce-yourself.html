Q01 Speaking Practice
01

Introduce
Yourself

Q. Please introduce yourself.

I’m a diet coach.
Most of my clients are women over forty.

STEP 01

全文を聞こう

まずは止めずに最後まで聞いて、全体の流れをつかもう。

I’m a diet coach.
Most of my clients are women over forty, and I work with them online. I’ve worked in the health and nutrition field for over ten years.

I lived in Phuket, but I moved to Bali two months ago to enjoy a laid-back lifestyle.

I love teaching people how to eat well and feel good in their bodies. I also like visiting local markets and learning about Balinese herbs and food.
Sometimes I write articles or make videos about diet and gut health.

STEP 02

チャンクごとに滑らかに読めるように練習しよう

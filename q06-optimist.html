06

An
Optimist?

Q. Are you an optimist?

Well, it depends
on the situation.

STEP 01

全文を聞こう

まずは止めずに最後まで聞いて、全体の流れをつかもう。

Well, it depends on the situation.
I wasn’t a very optimistic person before, but I became more optimistic after I started living abroad.
When I face problems, I try to find the positive side of things.
For example, the other day I missed the bus, but a kind local person helped me out.
At the same time, I’m more careful when I work, so I’m not always optimistic.

STEP 02

チャンクごとに滑らかに読めるように練習しよう

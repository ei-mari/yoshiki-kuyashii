Q. What are you hooked on?

I’m hooked on
making jamu.

STEP 01

全文を聞こう

まずは止めずに最後まで聞いて、全体の流れをつかもう。

I’m hooked on making jamu.
I started making it three months ago after coming to Bali.
I use ginger, turmeric, lemongrass, honey, and lime.
I first made it when I was sick and I immediately felt better.
Even my son drank it, and his headache went away.
I like it because it’s natural and healthy.
It’s a fun hobby, and I try new recipes every week.

STEP 02

チャンクごとに滑らかに読めるように練習しよう

Q. What’s been a constant activity in your life for a long time?

I’ve always loved
cooking.

STEP 01

全文を聞こう

まずは止めずに最後まで聞いて、全体の流れをつかもう。

Since I was little, I’ve always loved cooking.
As a kid, I loved helping in the kitchen.
Then as a teenager, I tried many new recipes.
Now, I teach people about healthy eating.
Last year, I started my online gut-health diet program.
It has changed many women’s lives.
It helps me feel happy and proud.
Even when I’m busy, I always find time to cook and learn more.

STEP 02

チャンクごとに滑らかに読めるように練習しよう

全文音声から切り出した音声を聞いて、同じリズムで読もう。

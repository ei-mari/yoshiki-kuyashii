Everyday

Q. What’s your everyday routine?

I drink herbal tea
every morning.

STEP 01

全文を聞こう

まずは止めずに最後まで聞いて、全体の流れをつかもう。

I drink herbal tea every morning because it wakes me up.
I usually eat fruit first to help with my digestion.
At night, I meditate instead of using my phone.
I've noticed that what I eat really affects my mood.
This month, I've been reading a lot about gut health.
On weekends, I go on day trips with my son.
These routines help me relax.

STEP 02

チャンクごとに滑らかに読めるように練習しよう
